Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my progress?

You can reset your progress by going to Settings > Reset Progress. Please note that this action cannot be undone.

Is there a way to disable sound?

Yes, you can disable sound effects and music in the Settings menu.

How do Game Center achievements work?

Make sure you're signed into Game Center on your device. As you play and reach certain milestones, achievements will automatically unlock.

The game is crashing on startup

First, try restarting your device. If the problem persists, check if your iOS is up to date, and make sure you have the latest version of Prime Collapse installed.

App Version Information

When contacting support, please include your app version and device model to help us troubleshoot more effectively.

You can find this information in Settings > About.
